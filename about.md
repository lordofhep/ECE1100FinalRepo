# About Me

### Professional Overview
I am an undergraduate based in Atlanta, GA, specializing in data research engineering and AI safety. I am highly adaptable and always eager to increase my practical exposure to current research. My experience spans leading operations for collegiate AI safety initiatives to collaborating on machine learning-based astrophysics projects. 

### Core Competencies
* **Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning:** Building predictive models, multi-agent coordination, and advancing AI safety.
* **Data Science:** Collating complex datasets, deriving analytical results, and utilizing advanced tools.
* **Engineering Research:** Modern high-energy physics and electrical engineering.
* **Leadership & Communication:** Mentoring peers, collaborating with multigenerational teams, and influencing international AI policy.

### Beyond the Lab
When I am not focused on software engineering or AI research, my interests expand into hardware and mechanical design. I spend time optimizing aerodynamic models for F1 in Schools using CFD simulations, and I enjoy diving into computer architecture, specifically RISC-V and VHDL. Outside of academics, I am actively learning Korean and stay disciplined by working toward my personal fitness and weight training goals.
